CRITICISM OF ERRONEOUS VIEWS:

In this context, the view that all (things and beings) are the substance of Brahman cannot be accepted, since sentient and non-sentient things are known by the pram─Бс╣Зas.to be entirely different from one another. DEPENDENT ON THE GRACE OF BHAGAV─АN.
